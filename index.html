I'm Hannah, a first-year interior design student based in Bengaluru. I'm drawn to spaces that feel lived-in and purposeful — where every material choice and spatial decision tells a story.

This portfolio grows alongside my education. Come back each semester to see how my work evolves from concept boards to full spatial designs.
